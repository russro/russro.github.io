## Overview

Built and operates an autonomous AI agent platform at FenixCommerce that coordinates specialized agents across four production systems serving multiple retail clients.

## Systems

### Delivery Date Prediction

Replaced the production delivery date prediction system. Tested 15 model architectures and validated across 78 temporal folds. *+25% on-time delivery improvement (+60% relative) across 5.96M orders.*

### A/B Test Incrementality

Measures where the delivery prediction widget helps vs. hurts revenue, then optimizes placement per geographic segment. *$4.93M incremental revenue identified across 7 retailers (+43% over unoptimized baseline).* Automated pipeline with experiment tracking and reporting.

### AI Sales Operations

Automated deal detection and outreach management for the sales team. AI-based deal classification, prioritized daily digests, and multi-stage follow-up cadence management.

### Delivery Performance Monitoring

Automated daily alerting across all fulfillment centers and key retailers. Detects anomalies and performance drops, generates AI-ranked business insights, and delivers summary email digests.

## Agent Platform

The coordination layer that ties everything together: command and control via messaging, institutional memory, task dependency management, experiment tracking, and structured protocols for deployment, reporting, and session handoff.
