**Feb – May 2023** \| Mentors: [Gav Sturm](https://www.linkedin.com/in/gabriel-gav-sturm-551666147/), [Wallace Marshall](https://cellgeometry.ucsf.edu/)

Built an image processing pipeline and trained a GAN to generate synthetic mitochondria images for downstream dynamics modeling. ~600 real images produced ~30,000 synthetic samples (50x).
