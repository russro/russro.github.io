**2025 – present** \| Advisor: [Reza Abbasi-Asl](https://abbasilab.org/) (UCSF / UC Berkeley)

Measuring whether clinical trials adopt published recommendations for broadening patient eligibility across 500K+ trials. Combines automated paper discovery, recommendation extraction, trial matching, and adoption assessment.

- Eligibility criteria tracking across 500K+ trials with 800K+ version snapshots
- Adoption gap analysis: 19K+ assessments across 658 recommendations and 4,400+ trials
- Paper discovery and extraction from 7,400+ papers (495 with verified recommendations)
- Trial matching using biomedical embedding models
- LLM benchmark with retrieval-augmented generation
