**Oct 2020 – Jul 2022** \| [Novartis article](https://live.novartis.com/article/how-a-drug-target-challenge-gave-rise-to-a-powerful-technology-platform/intro)

Designed and built an electrical stimulation system for cardiovascular drug screening on 96-well plates. Previous system only used 8 of 96 wells due to stimulator size and electrode limitations.

Solution: demultiplexing + solid-state switching (triacs) to route signals independently to each well, with a PCB designed for concurrent microscope imaging and biocompatible platinum/gold-coated electrodes.

- 8 channels to 96 channels (12x improvement)
- ~$2,000 to ~$200 per unit (10x cost reduction)
- Platform expanded to other cardiovascular research groups at Novartis
